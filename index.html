FAQ

How many members can be in a team ?

Teams for WieHack 4.0 can have 1-4 members and male students can also be part of the team provided minimum 50% are female members in a team.

When will it take place ?

WIEHack 4.0 will be held on 23th-24th May, 2023 . It will be a 36 hour hackathon.

Is it an offline hackathon ?

WIEHack 4.0 will take place in Offline mode as well as Online mode parallelly.Only first 70 shortlisted teams opting for offline mode will be called to the college premises.

Can outside Delhi students participate ?

Yes , participants all across the globe are welcome and can participate through online mode but the first 70 shortlisted participants must come to the college premises for the offline hackathon.

Will all the teams who have registered be selected ?

All teams registering for the event will be accepted but only the first 70 teams applying for offline would be allowed, rest of the teams would compete online.

How many rounds will be there ?

There will be 4 rounds :

  • Round 1 : Participants will give an abstract and presentation about their project followed by a mentoring round.
  • Round 2 : Shortlisted teams will start working on the the prototypes of their respective projects and explain the same.
  • Round 3 : After results of round the shortlisted teams will draft a business plan for their respective ideas.
  • Final round: The finalists will be required to give a full-fledged presentation,elaborately discussing their idea.

What are all the themes for this hackathon ?

There are total of 6 themes. Teams can choose any of these themes :

  • Women safety
  • Health and fitness
  • FinTech
  • Cyber security
  • Civil security
  • Open innovation

Is there any restriction on programming language or platform used for project ?

There is no restriction on the programming languages and platforms used to develop the product.

Where will this hackathon happen for offline participants?

WIEHack 4.0 is organised by the IEEE Student branch of Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, New Delhi. Hackathon will take place in college premises only.
